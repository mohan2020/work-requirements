Adding a new official form

  1. Drop the official DHS PDF into assets/ (e.g. PA_Medical_Frailty_official.pdf)
  2. Add an entry to assets/forms-manifest.json with templatePath and schemaId
  3. Add questionnaire fields in js/form-schemas.js if needed
  4. Add appToPdf mappings in js/pa-*-field-map.js and register in js/form-field-mapper.js
  5. Click Re-scan on this page — unmapped PDF fields and app-only fields will appear below
